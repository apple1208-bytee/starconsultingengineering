## Flexible Engagement Models

We tailor our consultancy approach to match the specific contractual and technical needs of your project, offering several distinct models of engagement.

![Man standing in front of people](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1542744173-8e7e53415bb0)

**Owner's Engineer:** We act as an extension of the facility owner's technical team. In this role, we oversee the primary EPC contractors, ensuring that the owner's specifications, quality standards, and schedule requirements are strictly adhered to throughout the detailed engineering and construction phases.

**Independent Engineer:** For financial institutions, lenders, and regulatory bodies, we provide an unbiased, third-party assessment of project viability. We evaluate technical feasibility, verify CAPEX/OPEX estimates, and assess technological risks to ensure the project represents a sound, executable investment.

**Third-Party Review:** We conduct rigorous, peer-level audits of complex engineering designs - such as advanced dynamic stress models or critical safety system architectures - to verify compliance with codes, validate assumptions, and identify potential design flaws before they manifest as costly construction or operational issues.

**Technical Due Diligence:** Prior to mergers, acquisitions, or major asset transfers, we perform deep-dive technical evaluations of existing infrastructure. We assess the remaining life of critical piping systems, evaluate maintenance records, and identify latent liabilities that could impact asset valuation.
